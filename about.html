About PM TECHNO HUBB

Karvenagar, Pune · We Build Intelligence

PM TECHNO HUBB is a technology education and innovation platform based in Pune, dedicated to empowering students with practical skills in Robotics, IoT, Electronics, Embedded Systems, and STEM learning. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world applications through hands-on workshops, project-based learning, and innovation-driven programs.

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers, innovators, and problem-solvers by providing immersive learning experiences that encourage creativity, critical thinking, and technical excellence. Through school partnerships, innovation labs, and industry-relevant training, PM TECHNO HUBB helps students build confidence, develop future-ready skills, and transform ideas into real-world solutions.

At PM TECHNO HUBB, we don't just teach technology—we empower students to build, create, and innovate.

Meet Our Founder

Praneel Mahatme

Founder & CEO

Praneel Mahatme is an innovation-driven entrepreneur and technologist passionate about democratizing STEM education in Pune. With expertise in IoT, embedded systems, and educational technology, he founded PM TECHNO HUBB to bridge the gap between traditional classroom learning and hands-on engineering skills.

His vision is to empower the next generation of engineers and innovators by providing accessible, project-based learning experiences that transform ideas into real-world solutions. Under his leadership, PM TECHNO HUBB has trained 100+ students, partnered with 15+ schools, and delivered 50+ workshops across Pune.
