At PM TECHNO HUBB, we believe true learning happens when theory meets practice. Located in Karvenagar, Pune, we equip students, schools, and businesses with cutting-edge technology skills.

From industrial IoT solutions like the Mobico CPM and UV Rakshak to inspiring robotics workshops for young minds — we bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world innovation.

Our mission: empower every student with practical skills to solve tomorrow's challenges using today's technology.
