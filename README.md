# PM TECHNO HUBB Website

Official website for **PM TECHNO HUBB**, a technology education and innovation platform based in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

## 🚀 About PM TECHNO HUBB

PM TECHNO HUBB is a technology education and innovation platform dedicated to empowering students with practical skills in:

* Robotics
* Internet of Things (IoT)
* Electronics
* Embedded Systems
* STEM Education
* Artificial Intelligence Tools
* Innovation & Project-Based Learning

We bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world applications through hands-on workshops, innovation labs, school partnerships, and industry-relevant training programs.

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers, innovators, and problem-solvers by providing immersive learning experiences that encourage creativity, critical thinking, and technical excellence.

> We don't just teach technology — we empower students to build, create, and innovate.
